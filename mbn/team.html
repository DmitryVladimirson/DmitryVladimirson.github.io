    Principle Data Science Consultant
    david@mbnsolutions.com

    David is a Data Science recruiter who works within London’s Machine Learning, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence community, with a particular focus on Computer Vision.

    Using his authentic, honest and consultative approach, he works in partnership to either augment his clients’ existing teams by supplying talent with a track record in industry or by building from the ground up by utilisinghis knowledge of leading Academic Institutions for the best grass roots Data Science, Machine Learning and AI talent.

    Whilst recruiting is a large part of David’s working day, he also coaches and mentorsothers within MBNs team on all aspects of Data Science practice.
