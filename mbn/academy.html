The bridge between academia and industry

MBN are passionate about nurturing a future generation of skilled Data & Tech professionals. And, through our Academy vehicle, we ensure that skills shortages in our core markets are recognised and addressed.

The Data Lab

As the Data Lab’s Delivery Partners for their MSc. Programme for the last five years, MBN Academy have placed almost 300 students into summer internships across Scotland’s private and public sector organisations whilst also delivering Employability training to over a thousand more aspirant Data & Technology professionals.

MBN Academy achieve their results through strong collaborations with a number of educational establishments and Focus Groups/Industry bodies where our knowledge and expertise can be contributed in an advisory capacity

  • UK Data & Marketing Association

    We are long-term contributors to The Data & Marketing Association’s Council on the subject of Skills & Talent and have been contributors to the DMA’s Creative Data Academy for a number of years where we train students in the latest Employability techniques used in the Data & Technology industries

  • SICSA

    MBN Academy are proud to support, and contribute to, the Scottish Informatics and Computer Science Alliance where we have presented on career trends and Employability to post-graduate Technology students.

  • SDS

    We have aided Skills Development Scotland’s understanding of the Data industry for a number of years now, regularly contributing Thought Leadership to their learning programmes and acting as advisors to their team wherever required.

  • Scotland’s AI Strategy

    MBN Academy were honoured to be asked to contribute to and participate in the Skills & Knowledge Working Group of this vital national research.

  • University of Glasgow

    Our Director of Academy sits on the Industrial Advisory Board of the University’s School of Mathematics & Statistics providing guidance on course content, industry trends and related topics.

  • Various Universities

    We have a strong track record of collaboration with a host of universities that includes Oxford, Cambridge, Regents and Hertfordshire. We have, for many years, assisted students with their understanding and pursuit of careers in the Data & Technology sectors.
