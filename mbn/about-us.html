We exist to help people succeed

The people who will help shape the future of the world though Data & Technology. At MBN we care about empowering people; helping people to fulfil their potential; and changing people’s lives for the better.

  • Established in 2017

    MBN Solutions were established in 2007 to address what we perceived to be a weakness within the recruitment industry and a lack of deep domain expertise in the areas of Data Science & Engineering, Analytics and Technology.

    13 years later, we are a hugely successful and market leading provider of Recruitment & People Solutions to disruptive and fast moving businesses seeking the very best Talent to support their strategic intent.

We do things the MBN way.

The MBN Way is a winning combination of:

– Extensive knowledge and unrivalled understanding of our markets – True professionalism with a service first mind-set

  • Leadership and passion

    MBN’s success has come about through a combination of leadership and passion. A passion to collaborate and build communities of stakeholders.

    From our partnerships with organisations such as The Data Lab to ensure the next generation of Data Talent has the Employability skills required for success to our hugely popular Meet-Ups and Webinars where Data & Technology enthusiasts can learn, collaborate and network - MBN Solutions are the partner of choice for any individual or organisation looking to ensure best in class Data, Analytics and Technology Recruitment Services.
