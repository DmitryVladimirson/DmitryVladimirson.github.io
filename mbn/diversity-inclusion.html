Diversity and Inclusion

MBN Solutions are wholly committed to ensuring that we provide as diverse and inclusive a pool of talent as possible to any opportunity that we work upon.
  • Search jobs
  • Contact us

Best fit, not first fit

We believe that diversity and inclusiveness within the candidate pools that we draw from provide our clients with the opportunity to hire the “best fit for the role” from as wide a field of applicants as possible. In addition, we believe that, as business leaders, we have a moral obligation to work to remove both conscious and unconscious barriers to entry, inclusion and promotion within the Data & Technology Industries.

  • What we’ve done

    To this end, we have worked hard to not only embrace but, through our actions and deeds, to promote both inclusivity and diversity within our community. Some of the tangible evidence of this commitment to inclusion and diversity in our sourcing methodologies, talent pool building and business operations include the following:

  • Talent4.0

    Talent4.0 looks to promote Diversity & Inclusion within the Scottish Data & Technology Industry by three routes: Inclusion, Connection and Progression. Our primary purpose is to act as a connector, logistical support and “Brand Amplifier” to the amazing work in Diversity & Inclusion that we have seen around us over the last few years.

Support of existing Diversity & Inclusion Initiatives
  • Sponsorship and participation in Scottish Woman in Technology forum

  • All profits from our highly successful “ScotChain” event were donated to a Social Enterprise called CodeFirst: Girls which is working to increase female participation in Coding related employment.

  • We have been long-term sponsors of the CoderDojo movement in Scotland. CoderDojo is a volunteer enterprise which works with children (with a particular focus on socially disadvantaged areas and female participation) who are interested in learning how to code.
    Read more
  • Code Your Future are another voluntary movement who teach Coding skills to refugees and migrants with the dual purpose of assisting with social integration and the promotion of Diversity within Technology populations. After speaking at one of our Talent4.0 initiative Meet-Ups we were delighted to be able to both promote their mission within our Community and to assist their Glasgow group with the use of our office and facilities for their weekly Meet-Up.

  • MBN Hosted Community Initiatives

    Our organisation and running of the Scotland Data Science & Technology, Scottish Blockchain and DevOps Glasgow Meet-Up Groups (free resources aimed at Community building within the Scottish Technology space with over 3,000 members).

    Scotland Data Science and Technology meet-up Scottish Blockchain meet-up DevOps Glasgow

  • Event Speaker Line-Ups

    We frequently feature female and BAME speakers at both our Meet-Ups and formal Events (our Data Scientist 2.0 and “Human & AI: Better Together” events, for example) as part of our corporate inclusion and diversity agenda. We believe that we have a platform that we should use to reflect the amazing Diversity that we see in Talent Pools within Data & Technology

    Data Scientist 2.0: Talent Driven Innovation - MBN Solutions & The Data Lab Human and AI, Better Together?

  • Individual Contributions by staff to Diversity & Inclusion activities

    Our Director of Academy & Client Services contributes to the Talent Hub of DMA Scotland. DMA have identified Neurodiversity as a key inclusion and diversity initiative for organisations within the Data and Marketing space and Rob works within a focus group who are looking at practical ways in which organisations can embrace NeuroDiversity within both their Talent Acquisition and Human Resource operations.

    DMA Scotland announce Talent hub Chair DMA Talent: Neurodiversity initiative

  • Academic Outreach with Primary Schools in Socially Disadvantaged areas

    We have, for a number of years, supported Primary Schools with visits and presentations designed to encourage young children to think about a potential career in Data & Technology. Typically, these schools are located in areas like Drumchapel and Castlemilk. We believe that it’s important that ALL children have an opportunity to meet with and talk to “Technology Professionals”.

    Read more

  • Recognition of our efforts towards promoting Diversity & Inclusion

    We were finalists in Data IQ’s 2018 Awards in the “Best Diversity in Data Programme” for our partnership with The Data Lab on their MSc. Placement Programme.

    Read more

    We believe that by staying active and visible as an organisation, championing inclusion and diversity and challenging exclusivity within our communities we can ensure that we set an inclusive and diverse example as an organisation and that we reach the widest pool of talent whilst supplying diversity to any resourcing project we undertake.
