Bringing technology people together

At Fruition, our dedicated consultancy team organize an oversee a range of networking events, both in the Yorkshire area and internationally to keep our finger on the pulse of thriving tech communities.

It is important that we connect with our markets so we can continue to provide a personal, specialized service to all our clients and candidates. Our meetups create a social environment in which technology professionals and enthusiasts can share knowledge and discuss key topics within the industry.
