Australia

The Parcll DDP service to Australia provides US eCommerce sellers with an excellent delivery solution from the US to the land down under.

With our DDP model, any package under AUD 1000 is exempt from import taxes and duties. After customs clearance, the Parcll local team in Australia injects packages directly to the Australia Post for delivery to consumers.

The service is fully tracked and the average transit time from US Hubs to Australian consumers is between 4-8 days.

Japan

Parcll operates a regular DDP service to Japan.

US eCommerce sellers reach Japanese consumers in an average 4-7 days from US Hubs and full tracking to door is guaranteed. The threshold for duty & VAT exempt clearance into Japan is at Y 16,666 which equals about USD 150 per package.

After customs clearance, the Parcll local team in Japan injects packages directly to Japan Post for last mile delivery across the country and Japanese consumers benefit from a highly localized delivery without any clearance concerns.

China

Parcll can arrange your eCommerce volumes into China via its dedicated DDP delivery solution that reaches over 1 Billion Chinese consumers.

DDP clearance for eCommerce into China is a challenge that we master via our dedicated Parcll teams on the ground.

Deliveries from US Hubs into China take about average 4-8 days to main cities and are fully tracked to door.
