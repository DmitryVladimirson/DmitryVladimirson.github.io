    Parcel Deliveries
Parcll belongs to YunExpress Group, one of the largest global eCommerce Logistics Solutions Providers.
Parcll is the easy solution for US Online Sellers to cross-border parcel deliveries. With Parcll, every parcel is sent DDP (delivered duty paid) and fully tracked to ensure that consumers always receive the best service they deserve.

We Understand Your Needs

YunExpress was founded in 2014, when a couple of eCommerce sellers realized that cross-border eCommerce needed better shipping solutions. As an answer, they founded YunExpress in Shenzhen, China (the Silicon Valley of Asia), a city where today 100% of busses and taxis run on electricity (the only such city in the world).

Since then, YunExpress Group has grown significantly and arranges today for more than 1 Mio cross-border deliveries daily. With its innovative global IT-Platform and over 100 contracts with last mile carriers across Europe, in the USA, Canada, Australia, Japan and others, YunExpress sets new standard in global cross-border deliveries tailored to the needs for eCommerce Sellers.

YunExpress is fully privately owned and managed by its founders.

Parcll is tailored to the needs of American Online Sellers that wish to grow their international sales to worldwide consumers.
