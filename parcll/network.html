Parcll Europe Delivery Partners

2,000,000 EU deliveries weekly

With Parcll you have access to the largest multi-carrier B2C delivery network in Europe with over 100 Mio. packages delivered yearly. Parcll clearance & distribution hubs in key EU countries and a Europe-wide trucking network dedicated to eCommerce guarantee for fast and steady flow of packages.

To ensure consumer-friendly localized deliveries, Parcll contracts directly over 60 last-mile carriers across the EU, such as Royal Mail UK, Hermes UK, Colissimo France, DHL Germany, Correos Spain, GLS Italy, Italy Post, DPD Austria, Inpost Poland and many more.

Parcll teams on the ground in the UK, Netherlands, Germany, France, Spain, Italy and many more countries ensure, that the distribution and return handling of your packages is rapid and reliable.

The Parcll network does not work with external agents, but arranges everything in-house. This way, we always know where your packages are. Full tracking on every package inclusive through our global IT platform.
