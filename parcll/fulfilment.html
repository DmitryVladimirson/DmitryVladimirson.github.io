Why Choose Parcll for your Fulfilment Customers?

We take pride in being strictly consumer-focused which is essential for eCommerce Sellers of any size. All of our shipping services are Delivered Duty Paid (DDP) and all shipping charges, Duty and Tax are fully known upfront, so that your fulfilment customers will never have to face unexpected surprises.

With Parcll, your customers can expect industry leading delivery times from the US to global consumers. Count on our reliable delivery times from the USA into Europe, Canada and many more global markets from 4 days up.

We ship over 1,000,000 packages per day through our global network and therefore achieve high economies of scale and large volume discounts with our selected destination last mile carriers, which allows you to offer your fulfilment customers the most competitive international shipping solution. We believe your customers always deserve low prices and a reliable service.

Affordable, Efficient, Transparent & On-Time

Parcll is fully tracked, therefore your customers know about the status of their package at all time. We deliver each package directly to the consumer’s doorstep, by the local carrier of their trust.

The delivery experience plays an important role in consumer satisfaction and consumer retention. It generates loyal consumers and increases your fulfilment customers’ brand value. Besides flawless pick & pack operations, shipping is the key element in providing your fulfilment customers with an outstanding logistics service and thus plays a pivotal role in the success of your 3PL business.
